Cuisine of Pune

Taste the flavors of Pune.

Local Food

Pune offers a delightful culinary experience with a mix of traditional Maharashtrian dishes and contemporary cuisines. Here are some must-try dishes:

  • Misal Pav: A spicy curry made of sprouts and served with pav.
  • Puran Poli: A sweet flatbread filled with lentil and jaggery mixture.
  • Bhakarwadi: A spicy, crispy snack that is a local favorite.
